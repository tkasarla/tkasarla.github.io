# Coffee

I enjoy brewing with Aeropress and Mokapot (and sometimes espresso)! Inspired by coffee pages from other researchers: [Justine Sherry](http://www.justinesherry.com/coffee.html){: target="_blank"}, [Divyansh Kaushik](https://www.cs.cmu.edu/~dkaushik/index.html){: target="_blank"}, [Akshay Narayan](https://akshayn.xyz/coffee.html){: target="_blank"}.

## Amsterdam ☕︎

- [Rum Baba Coffee Roasters](https://rumbabacoffeeroasters.com/){: target="_blank"} is my favorite in Amsterdam.
- [Simon Levelt](https://www.simonlevelt.nl/){: target="_blank"} has a large selection of good quality coffees and teas.

## India ☕︎

Note: [Kerehaklu](https://www.kerehaklu.com){: target="_blank"} — a 70-year-old estate in Chikmanglur — supplies coffee to multiple roasters across India. I've sampled most of their coffees and loved them!

### Roasters

- [Third Wave Coffee Roasters](https://www.thirdwavecoffeeroasters.com/){: target="_blank"}
- [Blue Tokai Coffee Roasters](https://bluetokaicoffee.com/){: target="_blank"}
- [Toffee Coffee Roasters](https://www.toffeecoffeeroasters.com/){: target="_blank"}
- [KC Roasters](https://kcroasters.com/){: target="_blank"}
- [VS Mani Co.](https://www.vsmanico.com/){: target="_blank"} — instant coffee worth trying.

### Cafes

#### Bangalore

- [Blue Tokai](https://bluetokaicoffee.com/){: target="_blank"} — wonderful sourdough from Sour House India and delicious open toasts.
- [Third Wave Coffee Roasters](https://www.thirdwavecoffeeroasters.com/){: target="_blank"} — classic bagels are a perfect pairing for their coffee.
- [Dyu Art Cafe](https://www.dyuartcafe.com/){: target="_blank"} — beautiful interiors; their scrambled eggs are light and fluffy!
